## 1. Typological Foundations

### 1.1 Core Typological Features

- **Analytic/isolating morphology**: Minimal inflection; grammatical relations marked by word order and particles
- **Topic-prominent language**: Topic-comment structure often supersedes subject-predicate
- **Pro-drop**: Pervasive null arguments (subjects, objects) recovered from context
- **Head-final tendencies in NP**: Modifiers precede head nouns (Dem-Num-Cl-Adj-N)
- **Head-initial tendencies in VP**: V-O order; complements follow verbs
- **No grammatical agreement**: No subject-verb agreement, gender, case marking, or number agreement
- **Classifier obligatory**: Numeral-classifier constructions required for noun quantification

### 1.2 Theoretical Frameworks Relevant to Analysis

- Li & Thompson (1981) descriptive grammar
- Huang, Li & Li (2009) generative syntax
- Chao Yuen Ren (1968) structural analysis
- Shi Dingxu functional approaches
- Construction Grammar approaches to resultatives and disposal constructions

## 2. Word Classes (词类)

### 2.1 Major Categories

#### 2.1.1 Nouns (名词)

- Common nouns (普通名词)
- Proper nouns (专有名词)
- Time nouns (时间名词): 今天, 明年, 目前
- Place nouns (处所名词): 这里, 国内
- Direction/localizer nouns (方位词): 上, 下, 里, 外, 前, 后, 中, 内, 间, 旁
- Bound localizers vs. free localizers

#### 2.1.2 Verbs (动词)

- Action verbs (动作动词)
- Stative verbs (状态动词)
- Psych verbs (心理动词): 喜欢, 担心, 害怕
- Modal verbs (能愿动词/助动词): 能, 会, 可以, 应该, 必须, 要, 得(děi), 愿意
- Existential/presentative verbs: 有, 是, 在
- Coverbs/prepositions (介词): 在, 从, 往, 向, 对, 给, 把, 被, 比, 跟, 用, 为
- Light verbs: 加以, 予以, 进行, 给予, 给以

#### 2.1.3 Adjectives (形容词)

- Gradable adjectives: 大, 好, 漂亮
- Non-gradable/absolute adjectives: 真, 假, 主要
- State adjectives (reduplicated forms): 干干净净, 漂漂亮亮
- Nominal adjectives (区别词/属性词): 男, 女, 主要, 公共, 高级

#### 2.1.4 Adverbs (副词)

- Degree adverbs: 很, 非常, 太, 最, 更, 比较, 稍微, 极, 颇
- Scope adverbs: 都, 全, 只, 仅, 也, 还, 又, 再
- Time/frequency adverbs: 已经, 曾经, 正在, 刚, 将, 常常, 往往, 总是
- Manner adverbs: 渐渐, 忽然, 悄悄
- Negation adverbs: 不, 没(有), 未, 别, 莫, 勿
- Epistemic/modal adverbs: 大概, 也许, 一定, 肯定, 似乎, 恐怕
- Conjunctive adverbs: 却, 倒, 反而, 于是, 因此, 从而

### 2.2 Minor Categories

#### 2.2.1 Pronouns (代词)

- Personal pronouns: 我, 你, 您, 他, 她, 它, 我们, 咱们, 你们, 他们
- Demonstratives: 这, 那, 此, 彼, 该
- Interrogatives: 谁, 什么, 哪, 哪里/哪儿, 怎么, 怎样, 为什么, 多少, 几
- Indefinites: 某, 有的, 有些, 一些, 任何, 每, 各
- Reflexive: 自己, 本身, 自身
- Reciprocal: 彼此, 互相

#### 2.2.2 Numerals (数词)

- Cardinals: 一, 二/两, 三... 十, 百, 千, 万, 亿
- Ordinals: 第一, 第二... (第 + cardinal)
- Approximate numbers: 几, 多, 来, 左右, 上下
- Fractions, decimals, percentages: 三分之一, 百分之五十, 零点五

#### 2.2.3 Classifiers/Measure Words (量词)

- Individual classifiers: 个, 位, 只, 条, 张, 本, 件, 把, 辆, 棵, 座, 所, 家, 种, 类
- Collective/group classifiers: 对, 双, 群, 批, 套, 系列
- Partitive classifiers: 块, 片, 段, 节, 滴
- Container classifiers: 杯, 瓶, 碗, 盒, 箱
- Standard measure words: 斤, 公斤, 米, 公里, 小时, 天, 年
- Verbal classifiers (动量词): 次, 遍, 趟, 下, 回, 顿, 番, 场, 阵

#### 2.2.4 Particles (助词)

- Structural particles: 的, 地, 得
- Aspect particles: 了, 着, 过
- Sentence-final particles: 了, 吗, 呢, 吧, 啊/呀, 嘛, 罢了, 而已
- Nominalizers: 的, 所, 者

#### 2.2.5 Conjunctions (连词)

- Coordinating: 和, 与, 及, 跟, 同, 或, 或者, 还是, 而, 并, 且
- Subordinating: See §13 Complex Sentences
- Correlative pairs: 虽然...但是, 不但...而且, 如果...就, 因为...所以

#### 2.2.6 Interjections (叹词)

- 啊, 哎, 哎呀, 唉, 嗯, 哦, 哼 (more common in dialogue representation)

## 3. Morphology and Word Formation

### 3.1 Compounding (复合)

- **Coordinate compounds** (并列式): 国家, 道路, 语言, 买卖
- **Modifier-head compounds** (偏正式): 火车, 白菜, 热心
- **Verb-object compounds** (动宾式/支配式): 吃饭, 睡觉, 开车, 说话
- **Verb-complement compounds** (动补式): 看见, 听懂, 打破, 提高
- **Subject-predicate compounds** (主谓式): 地震, 年轻, 心疼, 头疼

### 3.2 Affixation

#### 3.2.1 Prefixes

- 阿-: 阿姨 (kinship, names)
- 老-: 老师, 老虎, 老三
- 小-: 小王, 小心
- 第-: 第一, 第二 (ordinals)
- 初-: 初一, 初步
- 可-: 可爱, 可靠, 可见
- 非-: 非法, 非正式
- 反-: 反对, 反常
- 超-: 超市, 超级
- 多-: 多元, 多方面
- 单-: 单一, 单方面

#### 3.2.2 Suffixes

- -子: 桌子, 孩子, 本子
- -儿: 花儿, 事儿, 这儿 (erhua)
- -头: 石头, 木头, 想头
- -们: 我们, 人们, 孩子们 (plural, restricted)
- -性: 可能性, 重要性
- -化: 现代化, 全球化
- -者: 学者, 读者, 作者
- -家: 科学家, 作家
- -员: 演员, 成员
- -式: 中式, 正式
- -度: 温度, 程度, 高度
- -感: 美感, 责任感
- -观: 世界观, 人生观
- -论: 进化论, 相对论
- -学: 语言学, 哲学

### 3.3 Reduplication (重叠)

#### 3.3.1 Noun Reduplication

- AA: 人人, 天天, 处处, 年年 (distributive/universal reading)
- AABB: (rare for nouns)

#### 3.3.2 Verb Reduplication

- AA: 看看, 想想 (delimitative aspect: "do a little," tentative)
- ABAB: 研究研究, 考虑考虑
- A一A: 看一看, 想一想
- A了A: 看了看
- AABB: 说说笑笑, 走走停停 (iterative, casual)

#### 3.3.3 Adjective Reduplication

- AA的: 好好的, 慢慢的 (intensifying/manner)
- AABB: 高高兴兴, 干干净净 (vivid, intensified state)
- ABB: 绿油油, 红彤彤, 亮晶晶 (vivid description)
- ABAB: (restricted to certain disyllabic adjectives): 雪白雪白, 通红通红
- A里AB: 糊里糊涂, 慌里慌张 (pejorative connotation)

### 3.4 Abbreviation and Acronyms

- Coordinate reduction: 中国 + 美国 → 中美
- Numerical abbreviation: 三个代表 → 三代表 type
- Alphabetic acronyms: WTO, GDP, AI (borrowed)

## 4. Noun Phrase Structure

### 4.1 Basic NP Template

```
(Topic) + (Dem) + (Num) + (Cl) + (Adj/Modifier + 的) + Noun + (Localizer)
```

### 4.2 Demonstrative-Numeral-Classifier Sequences

- **Dem + Cl + N**: 这本书 ("this book")
- **Num + Cl + N**: 三本书 ("three books")
- **Dem + Num + Cl + N**: 这三本书 ("these three books")
- Classifier required with numerals and demonstratives; bare nouns unmarked for number

### 4.3 Modification with 的

#### 4.3.1 的 as Modifier Marker

- **Possessive**: 我的书, 中国的经济
- **Relative clause**: 我买的书 ("the book that I bought")
- **Adjectival**: 漂亮的女孩 (的 optional with monosyllabic adjectives: 大书 vs. 大的书)
- **Nominal modifier**: 木头的桌子, 历史的问题

#### 4.3.2 的 Omission Conditions

- Monosyllabic adjective + noun: 好人, 大事
- Close possession (inalienable, institutional): 我妈妈, 我们学校
- Fixed expressions: 中国人, 英语课

#### 4.3.3 Headless NPs / Nominalization

- 的-nominalization: 红的 ("the red one"), 我买的 ("what I bought")
- 所-nominalization: 所见, 所闻, 所需 (literary)
- 者-nominalization: 劳动者, 学习者

### 4.4 Relative Clauses

- **Prenominal only**: [Relative clause + 的] + Head noun
- **Gapless relatives** (Chinese allows): 他写字的样子 ("the manner [in which] he writes")
- **Head-internal relatives**: Limited constructions

### 4.5 Appositive Structures

- N1 + N2: 首都北京 ("the capital, Beijing")
- Restrictive vs. non-restrictive appositives

### 4.6 Localizers and Place Phrases

- **Simple localizers**: 上, 下, 里, 外, 前, 后, 左, 右, 中, 内, 间, 旁
- **Complex localizers**: 上面/上边/上头, 里面/里边/里头, 以上, 以下, 以内, 以外, 之上, 之下, 之中, 之间
- **NP + Localizer**: 桌子上 ("on the table"), 房间里 ("inside the room")

### 4.7 Quantification

- Universal: 所有, 一切, 全部, 每, 各
- Existential: 有的, 有些, 一些, 某
- Distributive reduplication: 人人, 个个, 处处, 天天

## 5. Verb Phrase Structure

### 5.1 Basic VP Template

```
(Adverb) + (Neg) + (Aux/Modal) + (Coverb Phrase) + Verb + (Complement) + (Aspect) + (Object)
```

### 5.2 Auxiliary/Modal Verbs (能愿动词)

| Modal   | Core Meaning                           | Notes                               |
|---------|----------------------------------------|-------------------------------------|
| 能       | ability (learned/inherent), permission | Negated: 不能                         |
| 会       | acquired skill, probability            | 不会                                  |
| 可以      | permission, possibility                | 不可以 (prohibition)                   |
| 要       | volition, necessity, future            | 不要 (prohibition) / 不用 (unnecessary) |
| 想       | desire, intention                      | 不想                                  |
| 应该      | obligation (deontic)                   | 不应该                                 |
| 必须      | strong necessity                       | 不必 (unnecessary) vs. 不用             |
| 得 (děi) | necessity (colloquial)                 | 不用 for negation                     |
| 敢       | dare                                   | 不敢                                  |
| 愿意      | willingness                            | 不愿意                                 |
| 肯       | willingness                            | 不肯                                  |

### 5.3 Coverbs/Prepositions (介词)

Coverbs introduce oblique arguments before the main verb:

| Coverb | Function               | Example |
|--------|------------------------|---------|
| 在      | location               | 在家工作    |
| 从      | source                 | 从北京来    |
| 往/向    | direction              | 往东走     |
| 对      | target, regarding      | 对他说     |
| 给      | benefactive, recipient | 给我看     |
| 跟/和/与  | comitative, comparison | 跟他说话    |
| 用      | instrument             | 用筷子吃    |
| 为/为了   | purpose, benefactive   | 为人民服务   |
| 把      | disposal (see §10)     | 把书放好    |
| 被      | passive (see §10)      | 被人看见    |
| 比      | comparison             | 比他高     |
| 按/按照   | according to           | 按计划进行   |
| 关于     | concerning             | 关于这个问题  |
| 通过     | through, by means of   | 通过考试    |
| 经过     | via, after             | 经过努力    |
| 随着     | along with             | 随着发展    |

### 5.4 Verb-Object Compounds and Separability

- **Separable verbs** (离合词): 睡觉 → 睡了一觉, 帮忙 → 帮他的忙, 见面 → 见个面
- Insertion of aspect markers, modifiers, classifiers between V and O

### 5.5 Pivotal Constructions (兼语式)

- NP1 + V1 + NP2 + V2
- 我请他吃饭 ("I invite him to eat")
- NP2 is object of V1 and subject of V2
- Common V1: 请, 让, 叫, 使, 派, 命令, 要求, 希望, 劝

### 5.6 Descriptive Complements with 得

- V + 得 + Complement (manner, degree, extent)
- 跑得快 ("run fast"), 说得很好 ("speak very well")
- 高兴得跳起来 ("so happy that [he] jumped up")

## 6. Sentence Structure and Constituent Order

### 6.1 Basic Sentence Types

#### 6.1.1 SVO Sentences

- 我看书 ("I read books")
- Default order for transitive predicates

#### 6.1.2 Topic-Comment Sentences

- **Topic + Comment**: 这本书我看过了 ("This book, I've read")
- **Hanging topic**: 大象鼻子长 ("Elephants, [their] noses are long")
- **Aboutness topics** vs. **moved topics**

#### 6.1.3 Subject-Predicate Predicate

- S + [S2 + VP]
- 他身体很好 ("He, [his] body is good" = "He is healthy")
- 这件事我不知道 ("This matter, I don't know")

#### 6.1.4 Existential/Presentative Sentences

- **Location + 有 + NP**: 桌子上有一本书
- **Location + V + 着 + NP**: 墙上挂着一幅画
- **NP + V + Location**: (less common)

#### 6.1.5 是-Sentences

- **Identificational**: 他是老师
- **Cleft/focus**: 是他打破的 ("It was he who broke it")
- **Affirmative assertion**: 他是来过 ("He DID come")
- **Existential with 是**: 到处是人

#### 6.1.6 有-Sentences

- **Possession**: 我有三本书
- **Existence**: 教室里有很多学生
- **Indefinite subject introduction**: 有人来了

### 6.2 Fixed Word Order Constraints

- **Time before place**: 我昨天在家看书 (Time-Place-Manner-V)
- **Adverbs before VP**: 他已经走了
- **PP/Coverb phrases before V**: 用筷子吃
- **Complements after V**: 跑得快, 看完, 走出去

### 6.3 Sentence-Final Position Effects

- New information typically in post-verbal position
- Definite/specific objects may prepose (with 把 or topic)

## 7. Aspect, Tense, and Modality

### 7.1 Aspect Markers (体标记)

Chinese has grammaticalized aspect but not tense; temporal reference is contextual or lexical.

#### 7.1.1 Perfective 了1 (Verbal 了)

- **Position**: Immediately after verb (V + 了)
- **Function**: Bounded, completed event; realization of action
- 我吃了饭 ("I ate" / "I have eaten")
- May co-occur with sentence-final 了

#### 7.1.2 Sentence-Final 了2

- **Position**: End of sentence
- **Function**: Change of state; currently relevant state; new situation
- 他来了 ("He has come" / "He's here now")
- 下雨了 ("It's raining now" = "It has started to rain")
- **了1 + 了2 combined**: 我吃了饭了 ("I've eaten now")

#### 7.1.3 Experiential 过

- **Position**: After verb (V + 过)
- **Function**: Experience at some indefinite past time
- 我去过中国 ("I have been to China [at some point]")
- Distinct from 了: focus on experience rather than completion

#### 7.1.4 Durative/Progressive 着

- **Position**: After verb (V + 着)
- **Function**: Ongoing state; continuous aspect
- 门开着 ("The door is open [state]")
- 他看着我 ("He is looking at me")
- Resultant state vs. progressive depending on verb semantics

#### 7.1.5 Progressive Markers

- **在 + V**: 在吃饭 ("is eating")
- **正在 + V**: 正在开会 ("is holding a meeting")
- **V + 着 + 呢**: 看着呢 ("is watching")
- **正 + V + 着 + 呢**: Full progressive marking

#### 7.1.6 Inchoative/Inceptive

- **V + 起来**: 笑起来 ("start laughing"), 热起来 ("start getting hot")
- **V + 下去**: Continuation: 说下去 ("continue speaking")
- **V + 下来**: Result + continuation: 停下来 ("stop [and remain stopped]")

### 7.2 Temporal Adverbs (Lexical Tense Marking)

- **Past**: 已经, 曾经, 刚, 刚才, 从前, 以前, 当时
- **Present**: 现在, 正, 正在, 目前
- **Future**: 将, 将要, 快要, 要, 会, 即将, 马上

### 7.3 Modality

#### 7.3.1 Epistemic Modality

- **Certainty**: 一定, 肯定, 必定, 准
- **Probability**: 大概, 也许, 可能, 恐怕, 似乎, 好像, 或许
- **Evidential-like**: 据说, 听说, 看来, 显然

#### 7.3.2 Deontic Modality

- **Obligation**: 应该, 应当, 必须, 要, 得(děi)
- **Permission**: 可以, 能, 准
- **Prohibition**: 不能, 不可以, 不准, 不许, 别, 禁止

#### 7.3.3 Dynamic Modality

- **Ability**: 能, 会, 能够
- **Volition**: 要, 想, 愿意, 肯

## 8. Complement Systems

### 8.1 Resultative Complements (结果补语)

- V + Result: 看见, 听懂, 打破, 写完, 做好, 弄清楚
- Lexicalized vs. productive combinations
- Potential form: V + 得/不 + Result (看得见, 看不见)

### 8.2 Directional Complements (趋向补语)

#### 8.2.1 Simple Directional Complements

- 来: movement toward speaker
- 去: movement away from speaker
- 上, 下, 进, 出, 回, 过, 起, 开

#### 8.2.2 Complex Directional Complements

- V + Direction + 来/去
- 走进来 ("walk in [toward speaker]"), 跑出去 ("run out [away]")
- Full paradigm: 上来/上去, 下来/下去, 进来/进去, 出来/出去, 回来/回去, 过来/过去, 起来

#### 8.2.3 Extended/Metaphorical Uses

- 想起来 ("recall"), 说出来 ("say it out"), 冷下来 ("cool down")
- 看出来 ("perceive/figure out"), 活下去 ("continue living")
- Inceptive 起来: 笑起来, 热闹起来
- Continuation 下去: 说下去, 坚持下去

### 8.3 Potential Complements (可能补语)

- **Affirmative potential**: V + 得 + Complement
- **Negative potential**: V + 不 + Complement
- 看得见 / 看不见 ("can see / cannot see")
- 吃得完 / 吃不完 ("can finish eating / cannot finish")
- 说得清楚 / 说不清楚 ("can explain clearly / cannot")

### 8.4 Degree/Extent Complements

- **V + 得 + Description**
- 累得很 ("very tired"), 高兴得跳起来 ("so happy [that one] jumped")
- Conveys extent, manner, or result of action

### 8.5 Quantity Complements (数量补语)

- **Duration**: V + Duration (看了三个小时)
- **Frequency**: V + Number + 次/遍/回 (去过两次)
- **Measure**: V + Quantity (走了十公里)

### 8.6 Locative Complements

- V + 在 + Location: 坐在椅子上, 放在桌子上
- V + 到 + Location: 走到门口, 寄到北京

## 9. Serial Verb Constructions

### 9.1 Definition and Properties

- Multiple verbs in sequence sharing arguments; no overt coordination
- Single intonation contour; shared TAM marking

### 9.2 Types of Serial Verb Constructions

#### 9.2.1 Purpose/Sequential

- V1 + V2 where V2 is purpose of V1
- 去买东西 ("go to buy things"), 出去吃饭 ("go out to eat")

#### 9.2.2 Manner

- V1 describes manner of V2
- 坐着看书 ("read while sitting"), 站着说话 ("speak while standing")

#### 9.2.3 Instrumental

- V1 indicates instrument for V2
- 用筷子吃饭 (though 用 is often analyzed as coverb)

#### 9.2.4 Consecutive/Cause-Effect

- V1 leads to V2
- 摔倒受伤 ("fell and got injured")

#### 9.2.5 Pivotal (兼语式)

- (See §5.5)
- 请他来, 让我看, 叫他走

#### 9.2.6 Descriptive

- V + V形容 manner
- 走过来, 跑回去 (with directional complements)

## 10. Voice and Argument Structure Alternations

### 10.1 把-Construction (Disposal Construction)

#### 10.1.1 Structure

- NP1 + 把 + NP2 + VP (+ Complement/Result)
- 他把门关上了 ("He closed the door")

#### 10.1.2 Constraints and Conditions

- Object NP2 must be **definite/specific** (known, identifiable)
- VP must be **complex** (result, direction, aspect, or other additional element)
- Conveys **disposal**: action affects/disposes of object
- Not merely transposition of SVO: pragmatic and semantic differences

#### 10.1.3 Obligatory 把 Contexts

- Resultative with locative: 把书放在桌子上
- Complex directional: 把他叫进来
- Certain idiomatic constructions

### 10.2 被-Construction (Passive)

#### 10.2.1 Structure

- NP1 + 被 + (NP2) + VP
- 他被(人)打了 ("He was hit [by someone]")

#### 10.2.2 Properties

- Agent (NP2) optionally retained
- Often conveys **adversative** meaning (unfortunate for subject)
- VP typically has result or aspect marking
- Formal/written variants: 为...所 (见§18)

#### 10.2.3 Related Passive Markers

- 叫: 叫人骗了 (colloquial, adversative)
- 让: 让他看见了 (colloquial)
- 给: 给偷了 (colloquial)
- 为...所: 为人所知 (literary)
- 受到: 受到批评 (formal)
- 遭到: 遭到拒绝 (formal, adversative)

### 10.3 Notional Passive (受事主语句)

- Object promoted to subject without 被
- 饭做好了 ("The food is ready [has been made]")
- 问题解决了 ("The problem has been solved")
- Requires appropriate context and verb semantics

### 10.4 Middle Voice / Unaccusative Constructions

- 门开了 ("The door opened")
- 杯子打破了 ("The cup broke")
- Subject is patient/theme; no agent expressed

### 10.5 Causative Constructions

- 使, 让, 叫, 令 + NP + VP
- 使人感动 ("move people"), 让他知道 ("let him know")
- Formal: 使得, 致使

## 11. Question Formation

### 11.1 Yes-No Questions

#### 11.1.1 吗-Questions

- Statement + 吗
- 你是学生吗？("Are you a student?")

#### 11.1.2 A-not-A Questions

- V-不-V: 去不去？是不是？喜欢不喜欢？
- V-没-V: 来没来？(for 了/过 contexts)
- Adj-不-Adj: 好不好？
- VO → V-不-VO or VO-不-V: 吃饭不吃？吃不吃饭？

#### 11.1.3 Tag Questions

- ...是吗？是不是？对吗？对不对？好吗？行吗？

### 11.2 Wh-Questions

#### 11.2.1 Wh-in-situ

- Wh-word remains in canonical argument position (no fronting)
- 你买什么？("What are you buying?")
- 谁来了？("Who came?")

#### 11.2.2 Wh-Words

| Word    | Meaning           | Notes                                   |
|---------|-------------------|-----------------------------------------|
| 谁       | who               |                                         |
| 什么      | what              | Also "any" in certain contexts          |
| 哪       | which             | Requires classifier: 哪本                 |
| 哪里/哪儿   | where             |                                         |
| 什么地方    | where             |                                         |
| 怎么      | how (manner), why |                                         |
| 怎样/怎么样  | how, what about   |                                         |
| 为什么     | why               |                                         |
| 多少      | how many/much     | For larger/unknown quantities           |
| 几       | how many          | For small expected numbers; requires Cl |
| 多 + Adj | how (degree)      | 多高, 多大, 多远                              |
| 什么时候    | when              |                                         |

#### 11.2.3 Non-interrogative Uses of Wh-Words

- **Indefinite/existential**: 我想吃点什么 ("I want to eat something")
- **Universal**: 什么都行 ("Anything is fine"), 谁都知道 ("Everyone knows")
- **Conditional**: 谁先来谁先吃 ("Whoever comes first eats first")

### 11.3 Alternative Questions

- A 还是 B？
- 你喝茶还是喝咖啡？("Do you drink tea or coffee?")

### 11.4 Rhetorical Questions

- 难道...吗？("Could it possibly be that...?")
- 怎么会...？("How could...?")
- 谁知道？("Who knows?" = "Nobody knows")
- 哪里/哪儿 + VP (negation): 我哪儿知道 ("I have no idea")

## 12. Negation

### 12.1 Standard Negators

#### 12.1.1 不 (bù)

- **Default negator** for states, habits, future, modals
- Negates: adjectives, stative verbs, 是, modal verbs, future actions
- 我不去 ("I won't go"), 不喜欢 ("don't like"), 不是 ("is not")

#### 12.1.2 没(有) (méi/méiyǒu)

- Negates **completed actions** (了) and **experience** (过)
- Negates **有** for existence/possession
- 我没去 ("I didn't go"), 没看过 ("haven't seen"), 没有钱 ("don't have money")
- Note: 没 + V, not *没 + V + 了 (了 incompatible with 没)

### 12.2 Other Negators

| Negator  | Usage                      | Register             |
|----------|----------------------------|----------------------|
| 别        | Negative imperative        | Colloquial           |
| 甭 (béng) | = 不用                       | Colloquial/dialectal |
| 未        | Not yet; literary negation | Written/formal       |
| 非        | Not; literary negation     | Written/formal       |
| 勿        | Prohibitive (don't)        | Literary             |
| 莫        | Prohibitive; indefinite    | Literary             |
| 无        | Without; not have          | Literary             |

### 12.3 Negation Scope

- Adverb placement affects scope: 我不都喜欢 ("I don't like all of them") vs. 我都不喜欢 ("I don't like any of them")
- 不 typically negates immediately following element

### 12.4 Double Negation

- 不能不 = "cannot not" = "must"
- 不得不 = "have no choice but to"
- 非...不可 = "must" (emphatic)

### 12.5 Negative Polarity Items

- 任何: 任何人都没来 ("Nobody came")
- 一点儿: 一点儿都不 ("not at all")
- 丝毫: 丝毫不 ("not in the slightest")

## 13. Complex Sentences

### 13.1 Coordinate Structures

#### 13.1.1 Conjunction (并列)

- 和, 与, 及, 跟, 同 (NP coordination)
- 并, 且, 而且, 并且 (VP/clause coordination)
- 又...又...: 又大又便宜 ("both big and cheap")
- 也...也...: 也不吃也不喝 ("neither eat nor drink")

#### 13.1.2 Disjunction

- 或, 或者, 还是
- 还是 for questions; 或者 for statements

### 13.2 Subordinate Structures

#### 13.2.1 Conditional (条件)

| Pattern         | Meaning                     | Notes                  |
|-----------------|-----------------------------|------------------------|
| 如果/要是...就...    | if...then...                | General conditional    |
| 假如/假设...就...    | if (hypothetical)...then... | More hypothetical      |
| 只要...就...       | as long as...then...        | Sufficient condition   |
| 只有...才...       | only if...then...           | Necessary condition    |
| 除非...否则/不然...   | unless...otherwise...       |                        |
| 万一...就...       | in case...then...           | Unlikely condition     |
| 即使/就是/哪怕...也... | even if...still...          | Concessive conditional |
| 不管/无论...都...    | no matter...still...        | Universal conditional  |

#### 13.2.2 Causal (因果)

| Pattern      | Meaning                           |
|--------------|-----------------------------------|
| 因为...所以...   | because...therefore...            |
| 由于...因此...   | due to...consequently... (formal) |
| 既然...就...    | since/given that...then...        |
| 之所以...是因为... | the reason...is because...        |

#### 13.2.3 Concessive (让步)

| Pattern         | Meaning             |
|-----------------|---------------------|
| 虽然...但是/可是/却... | although...but...   |
| 尽管...但是/仍然...   | despite...still...  |
| 即使...也...       | even if...still...  |
| 固然...但是...      | admittedly...but... |

#### 13.2.4 Purpose (目的)

- 为了...: 为了学习中文，他去了中国
- 以便...: 请早点来，以便我们准备 (formal)
- 以...: literary; 以示... ("in order to show...")
- 好...: colloquial; 快点，好赶上火车

#### 13.2.5 Result/Consequence (结果)

- 所以, 因此, 因而, 从而
- ...以致/以至于...: "so much so that," "to the point that"
- 结果...: "as a result"

#### 13.2.6 Temporal (时间)

| Pattern        | Meaning                     |
|----------------|-----------------------------|
| ...的时候 / 当...时 | when                        |
| 在...之前/以前      | before                      |
| 在...之后/以后      | after                       |
| 一...就...       | as soon as                  |
| 自从...以来        | since                       |
| 直到...才...      | not until...only then       |
| 趁...           | while (taking advantage of) |
| 每当...          | whenever                    |

#### 13.2.7 Comparison

- 比: A 比 B + Adj (+ Degree): 他比我高 ("He is taller than me")
- 没有: A 没有 B + Adj: 他没有我高 ("He is not as tall as me")
- 跟/和...一样 + Adj: 跟他一样高 ("as tall as him")
- 不如: A 不如 B: 我不如他 ("I'm not as good as him")
- 越...越...: 越来越好 ("better and better"), 越快越好 ("the faster the better")
- 越A越B: 他越说我越生气 ("The more he speaks, the angrier I get")

### 13.3 Correlative Constructions

- 不但...而且/还...: "not only...but also"
- 不是...而是...: "not...but rather"
- 一方面...另一方面...: "on one hand...on the other"
- 首先...其次...最后...: "first...second...finally"
- 宁可/宁愿...也不...: "would rather...than"
- 与其...不如...: "rather than...better to"
- 一边...一边...: simultaneous actions
- 又...又...: "both...and"
- 不是...就是...: "if not...then" / "either...or"

## 14. Information Structure

### 14.1 Topic and Focus

#### 14.1.1 Topic-Comment Structure

- **Sentence-initial topic position**
- Topic marked by: position, particles (呢, 嘛), pause
- 这件事，我不知道 ("This matter, I don't know [about it]")

#### 14.1.2 Topic Types

- **Moved topic**: 书我买了 (object fronted)
- **Base-generated topic**: 大象鼻子长 (hanging topic)
- **Aboutness topic**: 说到X... ("Speaking of X...")

#### 14.1.3 Focus Constructions

- 是...的 construction for focus:
    - Agent focus: 是他买的 ("It was he who bought it")
    - Time focus: 是昨天来的 ("It was yesterday that [he] came")
    - Manner focus: 是坐飞机去的 ("It was by plane that [he] went")
- 连...都/也...: Even-focus: 连他都不知道 ("Even he doesn't know")

### 14.2 Given vs. New Information

- **Given information**: Preverbal position (topic, subject, 把-marked NP)
- **New information**: Postverbal position (objects, existential subjects)

### 14.3 Contrastive Structures

- 不是A而是B: "not A but B"
- A不B: 好不好 (questioning), 说不说 (choice)
- 倒: 他倒聪明 ("He is clever, actually/contrary to expectation")
- 反而: 不但没好，反而更差了 ("Not only didn't improve, but got worse")

### 14.4 Definiteness and Specificity Effects

- Preverbal NPs: typically definite/specific
- Postverbal NPs: can be indefinite (有 sentences, presentatives)
- 把 requires definite object
- Bare nouns ambiguous; context-dependent

## 16. Cohesion and Discourse Coherence

### 16.1 Reference and Pro-drop

- **Pervasive null arguments**: Subject and object regularly omitted when recoverable
- **Zero anaphora**: 他来了。Ø 说 Ø 很忙。 ("He came. [He] said [he] was busy.")

### 16.2 Demonstratives for Discourse Reference

- 这: Proximal; current discourse topic
- 那: Distal; previously mentioned
- 此: Literary/formal "this"
- 该: "the said"; legal/formal contexts

### 16.3 Connectives and Discourse Markers

#### 16.3.1 Additive

- 而且, 并且, 同时, 另外, 此外, 再说, 况且, 何况

#### 16.3.2 Adversative

- 但是, 可是, 然而, 不过, 却, 倒, 只是

#### 16.3.3 Causal/Consequential

- 所以, 因此, 因而, 从而, 于是, 结果

#### 16.3.4 Sequential/Temporal

- 首先, 其次, 然后, 接着, 最后, 终于

#### 16.3.5 Exemplifying

- 例如, 比如, 譬如, 比方说

#### 16.3.6 Summarizing

- 总之, 总的来说, 简言之, 归根结底

### 16.4 Paragraph and Text Structure

- Topic sentences often initial
- 总-分-总 structure common (general-specific-general)
- Parallel structures (排比) valued stylistically

## 17. Register and Style Variation

### 17.1 Spoken vs. Written Register Distinctions

| Feature      | Spoken/Colloquial | Written/Formal |
|--------------|-------------------|----------------|
| Pronouns     | 这儿, 那儿            | 此处, 彼处         |
| Conjunctions | 和, 可是             | 与, 然而          |
| Negation     | 没                 | 未              |
| Passive      | 叫, 让, 给           | 被, 为...所       |
| "And"        | 和, 跟              | 与, 及           |
| "Because"    | 因为                | 由于, 鉴于         |
| Modal        | 能                 | 能够, 得以         |

### 17.2 Academic and Formal Written Style

- Preference for disyllabic and classical vocabulary
- Four-character expressions (四字格)
- Nominalization (V → N via 的, 性, 化)
- Passive constructions with 被, 受到, 遭到
- Complex sentence connectives
- Use of light verbs: 进行, 加以, 予以, 给予

### 17.3 Journalistic Style

- Headline compression
- Time-fronted sentences
- Quotation conventions: 他说："..." / 据报道...

### 17.4 Literary Style

- Greater tolerance for ambiguity
- Classical elements (see §18)
- Figurative language, parallelism
- Non-canonical word order for effect

### 17.5 Internet/Digital Register

- Abbreviations: 不知道 → 不造/bù zào
- Homophones/near-homophones for humor: 河蟹 = 和谐
- Code-mixing with English, Pinyin
- Novel particles and expressions

## Appendix A: Key Grammatical Tests for Ambiguous Constructions

### A.1 Tests for Aspect (了1 vs 了2)

- Can it co-occur with another 了? (了1 can precede 了2)
- Is it after the verb or sentence-final?
- Does negation use 没 (perfective) or 不 (non-perfective)?

### A.2 Tests for 把 vs. SVO

- Is the object definite? (把 requires definiteness)
- Is there a resultative/directional/aspect complement? (把 strongly prefers complex VP)

### A.3 Tests for Coverb vs. Main Verb

- Can the element take aspect markers? (Main verbs: yes; Coverbs: no)
- Can it stand alone as predicate? (Main verbs: yes; Coverbs: no)

### A.4 Tests for Adjective vs. Stative Verb

- In Chinese, often analyzed together as "stative verbs"
- True adjectives: can be modified by 很 without comparative meaning
- Verbal properties: can take negation 不, aspect markers (restricted)
